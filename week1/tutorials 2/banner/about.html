About Me

Educator • Developer • Music Enthusiast

Welcome to My World

I'm Curt Rode, an educator and web developer passionate about bridging the gap between technology and learning. With a background in both education and web development, I bring a unique perspective to creating engaging digital experiences.

Professional Background

Education

As an educator, I believe in the power of hands-on learning and practical application. My teaching philosophy centers on making complex concepts accessible and engaging for students of all backgrounds. I specialize in web development education, helping students build both technical skills and creative problem-solving abilities.

Web Development

My development work focuses on creating clean, accessible, and user-friendly web experiences. I'm passionate about modern web technologies, responsive design, and writing code that is both functional and maintainable. I enjoy working with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and various frameworks to bring ideas to life.

Teaching Approach

  • Hands-on, project-based learning
  • Real-world application of concepts
  • Encouraging experimentation and creativity
  • Building confidence through incremental success
  • Fostering collaborative learning environments

Beyond the Code

Music & Creativity

When I'm not coding or teaching, you'll likely find me exploring the intersection of music and technology. I'm particularly fascinated by how legendary musicians like Paul McCartney and Geddy Lee have embraced technology to enhance their creative expression. This passion for music often influences my approach to web development, where I see code as another form of creative expression.

Philosophy

I believe that the best learning happens when we combine structured knowledge with creative exploration. Whether it's writing code, teaching a class, or playing music, I'm driven by the idea that technology should enhance human creativity, not replace it.

Current Teaching

MLA 60970 - Web Development

This course introduces students to the fundamentals of web development, covering HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern web design principles. Students work through hands-on projects, building everything from simple web pages to interactive web applications.

Course Highlights:

  • Responsive web design techniques
  • Modern CSS layout methods (Flexbox, Grid)
  • JavaScript fundamentals and DOM manipulation
  • Version control with Git
  • Web accessibility best practices
